# Code of Conduct

This project is for building reliable AI middleware for business systems. We expect contributors to keep discussions technical, respectful, and actionable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct about risks, tradeoffs, and failures.
- Critique code and architecture, not people.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce issues.
- Remove secrets, private data, and customer data from examples and logs.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope, security, and project direction.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, customer data, or internal deployment details.
- Repeated off-topic or intentionally disruptive comments.
- Misrepresenting security impact or exploitability.

## Reporting

For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For conduct concerns in public project spaces, contact the maintainers through the repository owner contact channel.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
